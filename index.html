Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries, and Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia;

  • 4 cups(500gr ) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr)
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for pan

Fpr the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinkly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring of knife, though it's not necessary )
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. you don't need to actually knead thedough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the blowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
